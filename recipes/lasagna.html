Classic Lasagna

Description

This recipe will show you how to prepare a classic lasagna.

Follow these instructions to learn how to make it

Ingeredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese of your choosing
  • Marinara Sauce
  • beef

Steps

  1. Lay out pasta noodles in baking dish
  2. Start with layer of sauce
  3. Lay down all other ingeredients
  4. Fold over and layer noodles
